VaxHelp

An NLP powered FAQ Chatbot to help answer queries about the
Vaccination drive in India.

Problem Statement

COVID-19 has been prevalant for over a year now, and finally the vaccination drive for the same has been initiated.

The vaccination drive is being held in phases, with the frontline workers been given priority. This leaves people with questions over their eligiblity. Also, There are prevalant rumours about the vaccine and its effects which is spreading mass distrust, making it difficult for people to adopt to the vaccine.

Therefore, for the aforementioned reasons, it has become important for people to be connected to an official source of information regarding COVID-19 and its vaccination. To reduce the load on frontline workers, reduce panic caused by false information, and automate the process, A chatbot can be utilized.
